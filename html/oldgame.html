Waffle Boy's Jungle Adventure


This game, which was used to advertise the now discontinued Waffle Crisps (RIP), was surprisingly fun for a game based off cereal. Then again, online gaming site Millsberry (double RIP) was also pretty great.

Kim Possible - A Sitch In Time episode 01


Join Kim in the past, present, and future as she fights Shego and saves the world. Based on the hit Disney movie (which featured Ron and Kim kissing for the first time, you love to see it!!!!!), this game was instantly addicting.

Rainbow Unicorn Attack


There's really not much to say about this game. The graphics are pretty, the song's a jam, and the gameplay is simple. I could easily play this for hours.

The Classroom


I don't know who came up with the idea to make a game based off cheating in school, but something about playing it gave me an illicit thrill as a child. I vaguely remember a kid jumping out of the window at one point??? Wild.
